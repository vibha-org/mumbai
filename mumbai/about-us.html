      About Us

      Never doubt that a small, group of thoughtful, committed citizens can change the world. Indeed, it is the only thing that ever has. -- Margaret Mead

      We are Vibha Mumbai, the Mumbai action center for Vibha, a US-based, non-profit, non-political and non-religious organization. Our volunteers organize regular campaigns and events to promote our mission and raise funds to support child development projects in India.

      Community Involvement

      Vibha Mumbai volunteers regularly work with several projects that help underprivileged children in the Mumbai area. We organize special events through which we provide the opportunity for everyone to help the children in our community.
