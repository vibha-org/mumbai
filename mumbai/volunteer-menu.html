      Volunteers

      Volunteering with Vibha Mumbai

      Vibha is a non-profit that is completely volunteer driven; volunteers make Vibha. We are constantly looking for individuals who are motivated to educate and empower underprivileged children. Our volunteers work together to create a brighter future for thousands of underprivileged children in India. Volunteering for Vibha is also fun! It gives you the opportunity to meet new people, make new acquaintances, network, form new friendships, and fun socializing!

      In Mumbai we have partnered with CORP India and Children Toy Foundation. CORP India runs balwadis, study classes and shelters for children from Thane slum area, sex worker communities and street children of Mumbai central. Children Toy Foundation educates disadvantaged children through play, beyond reading and writing, so as to help them acquire real skills and develop positive attitudes in their life.

      Some planned activities are NGO visits, Monthly dental and eye camp for children of disadvantaged communities, Weekend camps, Children counseling, Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) partner activities, Vibha Mumbai branding/ marketing, and Partnership with schools and colleges.

      We are looking for volunteers for event planning and execution, IT, social media communications, volunteer relations, marketing and branding, building partnerships, and accounting.

      Every small step helps. Together we can create a brighter future!
