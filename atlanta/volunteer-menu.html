Volunteers

Volunteering with Vibha Atlanta

Vibha volunteers are at the heart of our organization. Our volunteers work together to create a brighter future for thousands of underprivileged children in India and the US. Most of the Vibha volunteers are working professionals in the US and some are also students at various colleges. Volunteering for Vibha is also fun! It gives you the opportunity to meet new people, make new acquaintances, network, form new friendships, and fun socializing! The Atlanta volunteers hang out and go to dinners, movies, parties, hikes, potlucks, picnics, cycling, watching/playing cricket, etc.

Every small step helps. Together we can create a brighter future!
