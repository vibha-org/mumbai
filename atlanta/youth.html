Youth

Objectives

 Vibha Youth is an initiative in Atlanta aimed at encouraging Middle and High School students in volunteering.

  • To involve youth in the cause of underprivileged children.
  • Provide Leadership opportunities for youth.
  • Provide Volunteering opportunities that count towards their school volunteering hours.
  • Provide a platform to do hands on volunteering locally (Atlanta Food-Bank, Hands on Atlanta, etc).
